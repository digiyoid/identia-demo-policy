Privacy Policy - Identia Demo App

Last updated: July 15, 2025

This application has been developed by Identia solely for internal demonstration purposes, to showcase its AI-based identity verification technology using computer vision techniques.

Data Usage

  • This app does not collect, store, or share any real personal user data.
  • All images used during demo flows are simulated or anonymized.
  • Data processed during the demo is temporarily handled and automatically deleted after the session ends.

Required Permissions

  • Camera access is required to capture simulated images of faces and identity documents, exclusively for demonstration purposes.
  • Internet access is required to securely connect to the Identia API over HTTPS.

Scope and Limitations

  • The app is intended for use only in controlled environments and by users authorized by Identia.
  • It is not designed for commercial use or for processing real customer information.

Responsibility

Identia applies industry best practices in security and privacy, even in testing environments. However, this app is not meant for production use unless explicitly customized and covered by a contractual agreement.
