Privacy Policy of Digiyo S.A.

Last updated: 01/30/2025

At Digiyo S.A. (hereinafter referred to as "the company"), we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you access and use our Digiyo app available in the iOS App & Google Store, in compliance with Law 6534/20 of Paraguay and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following personal information when you use our application:

  • Information you provide: This includes data such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide when you register or interact with the app.
  • App usage information: We collect data related to how you interact with the app, such as features used, screens visited, and time spent within the application.
  • Device information: Includes details such as device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, and other data related to access to our application.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage the functionality of the digital onboarding feature in our app and website.
  • To improve user experience and personalize interactions within the app.
  • To analyze app usage and improve service quality and efficiency.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data

The processing of your personal data is based on the following legal grounds, in accordance with Law 6534:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide your personal information to interact with our app.
  • Contract performance: To fulfill the necessary functions to provide you with our digital onboarding services.
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services and ensure the technical functionality of the app.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service providers: We may share your information with companies that assist us with technical services, analytics, or support, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our legal rights, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, please note that due to the nature of the internet, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information, including facial data, only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Facial data is collected to enable the digital onboarding functionality. We perform comparisons between captured images to generate a score that allows for secure identity validation. This information is retained only for a period of 6 months, in accordance with Law 6534, after which it is securely and permanently deleted. We do not retain facial data indefinitely.

7. Your Rights

In compliance with Law 6534, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of rectification: You may request that we correct or update your personal information.
  • Right of erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data from our records.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your personal data in certain cases.
  • Right to data portability: You may request that we transfer your data to another service provider in a structured, readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided at the end of this policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices or new legal requirements. Any changes will be published on this page with the corresponding update date.
